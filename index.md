## Genwakan 厳和館

Muso Jikiden Eishin Ryu Iaido classes for adults and youth in Alameda and Emeryville.
Genwakan is an official dojo of World MJER Iaido Federation 正統正流無雙直傳英信流居合道国際連盟 https://mjer-iaido.github.io/en/
